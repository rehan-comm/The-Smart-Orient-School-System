Welcome to Smart Orient

Building Bright Futures Since 2017

Smart Orient is more than just an educational institution — it's a nurturing community where curiosity meets opportunity. With a focus on holistic development, we empower students from Nursery to Class 10 to excel academically, grow emotionally, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our state-of-the-art campus, dedicated faculty, and value-based education create an environment where every child feels valued, challenged, and inspired to reach their full potential.

Our Classes & Departments

From foundational learning to advanced academics, we offer a structured journey for every age group with dedicated departments.

Age: 3–5 Years

Pre-Primary (Nursery & KG)

Play-based learning, storytelling, motor skills development, and joyful introduction to numbers, letters & colors in a nurturing environment.

Age: 6–10 Years

Primary (Classes 1–5)

Core subjects with hands-on activities, value education, arts, sports, and foundational skill-building for lifelong learning.

Age: 11–15 Years

Middle School (Classes 6–8)

In-depth curriculum in Science, Math, Social Studies, Languages with project-based learning and co-curricular activities.

Age: 16–18 Years

Senior Secondary (Classes 9–10)

Streams: Science, Commerce & Humanities with advanced labs, career guidance, competitive exam preparation & holistic development.
